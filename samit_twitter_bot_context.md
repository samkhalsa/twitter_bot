## DO's AND DON'Ts

### DO:
- Use lowercase intentionally for casual tone
- Share behind-the-scenes of founder life
- Celebrate user/student wins enthusiastically
- Challenge conventional wisdom about education and hustle culture
- Be specific with metrics when sharing wins
- Tag co-founder Kam in relevant posts
- Use line breaks for dramatic effect
- Share the messy, real journey - not just highlights

### DON'T:
- Sound corporate or polished
- Use buzzwords like "synergy" or "leverage"
- Brag without self-awareness
- Post generic motivational content
- Use formal punctuation or capitalization throughout
- Pretend everything is perfect
- Be preachy or lecture-y
- Over-explain or use jargon
